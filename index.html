How a Visit Typically Flows

  1. Step 1 – Book or Drive In

    Guests choose a wash package and, in a live funnel, can pre-order drinks before they arrive.

  2. Step 2 – Wash & Brew

    Cars enter the wash bay while guests settle into the café with their chosen drinks and snacks.

  3. Step 3 – Dry, Detail & Depart

    Cars are dried and prepped. Guests finish their coffee, review any add-ons, and head out refreshed.
